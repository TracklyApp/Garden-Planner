01 Getting Started

Garden Planner & Tracker is a single file that runs entirely in your browser — no install, no account, no internet connection required.

  1. Open garden-planner.html by double-clicking it, or dragging it into any browser window.
  2. Use the sidebar on the left to jump between sections — click the ☰ menu button on mobile to open it.
  3. Everything you type or upload is saved automatically to this browser's local storage as you go.
  4. The app comes pre-loaded with example entries in every section so you can see how it works — edit or delete them and start adding your own data.
💡

Tip — Because your data lives in this specific browser, export a backup regularly (see Backup, Import & Export) especially before clearing browser data or switching devices.

🔒

Preview mode — Free/preview copies of this file allow a limited number of changes before asking for an activation code. Your purchase includes the activation code — enter it once via the "Activate" link at the bottom of the page to unlock unlimited editing permanently in this browser.

03 Garden Layout

Map out where everything grows.

Garden Sketch

Upload a photo or drawing of your garden layout with the file picker. It's stored directly in the app — click 🗑️ Remove Sketch underneath the image to delete it.

Crop Positioning Notes

A free-text box for crop rotation plans, bed orientation, or sun exposure notes. Click Save Notes after editing.

Bed Dimensions

Click + Add Bed to record a bed's name, size, planned crop, and an optional photo. Bed names you create here automatically appear as choices in the Zone dropdown of the Watering Log.

⚠️

Companion planting check — Beds are treated as physically next to each other in the order they're listed. If a bed's crop is a bad companion for the crop in the next bed (per your Companion Planting Chart), both rows get a "⚠️ Bad neighbor" badge — hover it to see why.

04 The Garden Log Sections

Ten trackers covering every stage of the growing season. All work the same way:

  1. Click + Add … at the top right of the section.
  2. Fill in the form — fields marked with * are required.
  3. Click Save. Your entry appears instantly in the table.
  4. Use the ✏️ and 🗑️ icons on any row to edit or delete it.

05 Yield Summary

The only section that calculates itself.

Yield Summary automatically totals your Harvest Tracker quantities against every cost you've logged — Seed Inventory, Fertilizer Log, and Garden Expenses — to show total yield, total cost, and cost per kg, with a breakdown by source. It also pulls in Produce Valorization to show actual revenue and profit from anything you've marked as sold, plus a breakdown of where the rest of the harvest went. Enter an estimated sale price per kg and click Recalculate to see a separate projected revenue and profit if you sold your entire harvest at that price.

07 Backup, Import & Export

Your data lives in this browser only — these tools are how you keep it safe or move it elsewhere.

  1. Click ⬇️ Export in the top bar to download a full JSON backup file, named with today's date.
  2. Store that file somewhere safe (cloud drive, external disk).
  3. To restore or transfer your data, click ⬆️ Import and choose a previously exported JSON file.
⚠️

Careful — Importing a backup replaces all current data in the app. Export your current data first if you don't want to lose it.

08 Dark Mode & Printing

Two small buttons, both in the top bar.

🌙 / ☀️ toggles dark mode — your preference is remembered for next time. 🖨️ Print opens your browser's print dialog so you can print any section, or save it as a PDF.

09 Privacy & Data

Nothing you enter ever leaves your device.

Garden Planner & Tracker has no server, no account, and no analytics. All information — including uploaded photos — is stored in this browser's local storage. Clearing your browser's site data for this file will erase everything, so keep regular exports as described above.

10 Tips & FAQ

Can I use this on my phone or tablet?

Yes — the layout adapts to any screen size. On narrow screens, tap the ☰ icon to open the section menu.

Where did the demo entries come from?

Every section ships with a few example rows so you can see the intended format at a glance. Delete them with the 🗑️ icon whenever you're ready to start fresh.

I accidentally deleted something — can I undo it?

There's no undo, but if you exported a recent backup you can re-import it. Otherwise, deletions are permanent — the app will always ask you to confirm before deleting a row.

Does this sync between my devices?

Not automatically. Export a backup on one device and import it on another to move your data across.
